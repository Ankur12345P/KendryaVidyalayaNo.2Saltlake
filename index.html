Kendriya Vidyalaya No.2, Salt Lake is situated in a very serene locality opposite NiccoPark. The building is a beautiful piece of architecture built in an extensivearea covering 3.5 acres of prime land. It is located in the midst of a tranquilenvironment far from the maddening crowd. The facility of rickshaw,auto-rickshaw, bus and taxi is available near the school. Atpresent there are more than 1900 students and 76 staff members inpursuit of Teaching and Learning. In the session 2010-11, the Vidyalayawillhave four sections from classes I to X and two sections each in classesXI& XII Science and one section in Commerce Themagnificent three storeyed school building harbours all the requiredfacilities and amenities of a senior secondary school. The beautiful garden atthe entrance, the spacious playground and the separate play field for tiny-totsadd grandeur to the Vidyalaya.
